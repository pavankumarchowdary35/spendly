Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense records — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic log data such as login timestamps and page visits, used to keep the service running smoothly.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service you signed up for.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls to protect your information.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use limited third-party services to operate the platform (e.g., hosting providers), and these parties are contractually bound to protect your data.

We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other websites.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download the data associated with your account.
  • Correct inaccurate information at any time via your profile settings.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the support page. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
